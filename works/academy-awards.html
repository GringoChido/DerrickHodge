Symphonic · Oscars

94th Academy Awards

2022

Lead Orchestrator and Conductor for the 2022 Oscars. Orchestrator and Co-Conductor for the first All-African-American orchestra in Academy Awards history.

The 94th Academy Awards represented a watershed moment in the history of the Oscars. Hodge served as Lead Orchestrator and Co-Conductor, assembling and leading the first All-African-American orchestra ever to perform at the ceremony. The achievement was not merely symbolic — Hodge's orchestrations brought a depth and cultural resonance to the evening's musical performances that reflected the full breadth of American musical tradition.
