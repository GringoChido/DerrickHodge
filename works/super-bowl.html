Event · Super Bowl

Super Bowl LVI

2022

Arranged orchestra for the "Lift Every Voice" performance at Super Bowl LVI — a nationally broadcast moment that showcased the power of symphonic arrangement on the world's biggest stage.

The performance reached over 100 million viewers and demonstrated how orchestral music can amplify and elevate the emotional power of culturally significant moments. Hodge's arrangement was both reverent and forward-looking, honoring tradition while pushing the art form into new territory.
